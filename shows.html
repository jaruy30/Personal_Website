Adventure TV Shows


Adventure Time





Synopsis: Adventure Time follows the adventures of a boy named Finn and his best friend, a shape-shifting dog named Jake, in the post-apocalyptic Land of Ooo. Together, they embark on various quests and encounter colorful characters while battling villains like the Ice King.

Director: Pendleton Ward

Stars: Jeremy Shada (Finn), John DiMaggio (Jake)





Gravity Falls













Synopsis: Gravity Falls follows the adventures of twins Dipper and Mabel Pines as they spend their summer with their great-uncle Stan in the mysterious town of Gravity Falls, Oregon. They uncover supernatural mysteries and encounter strange creatures while trying to unravel the secrets of the town.

Director: Alex Hirsch

Stars: Jason Ritter (Dipper), Kristen Schaal (Mabel)

Comedy TV Shows


ICarly Show







Synopsis: iCarly revolves around Carly Shay, a teenager who creates her own web show with her best friends Sam and Freddie. The show becomes a huge hit, leading to various misadventures and challenges as they navigate teenage life while managing their newfound fame.

Director: Steve Hoefer

Stars: Miranda Cosgrove (Carly), Jennette McCurdy (Sam), Nathan Kress (Freddie).









Tom and Jerry





Synopsis: Tom and Jerry is a classic animated series featuring the comedic rivalry between Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse. Their slapstick antics lead to chaotic situations as they constantly outwit and outmaneuver each other in various settings.

Director: William Hanna, Joseph Barbera

Stars: Chloë Grace Moretz, Michael Peña, Colin Jost, Rob Delaney, Pallavi Sharda,.

Fiction TV Shows





Courage The Cowardly Dog



Synopsis: Courage the Cowardly Dog follows the adventures of Courage, a timid but kind-hearted dog who lives with his elderly owners, Muriel and Eustace Bagge, in the middle of Nowhere. Courage must often overcome his fears to protect his family from supernatural threats and bizarre occurrences.

Director: John R. Dilworth

Stars: Marty Grabstein (Courage), Thea White (Muriel), Lionel G. Wilson (Eustace).





Mr. Bean Animated





Synopsis: The Animated Series follows the misadventures of the iconic character Mr. Bean, who finds himself in various comedic situations as he navigates everyday life. His childlike antics often lead to chaos and hilarity, much to the frustration of those around him.

Director: Tim Searle

Stars: Rowan Atkinson.
