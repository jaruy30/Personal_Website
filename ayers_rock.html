Dream Places I want To Visit

AYERS ROCK





The indigenous Anangu people, who have inhabited the area for thousands of years, place a high value on Ayers Rock. My wish to see this precious place is made more important and meaningful by their spiritual ties to it. I intend to obtain a deeper understanding of the spiritual traditions and cultural history of Australia's Indigenous peoples by visiting Ayers Rock myself.

Ayers Rock provides an opportunity for meditation and a reconnection with nature at its most basic. With its wide stretches of red desert and starry nights, the raw beauty of Australia's Outback region offers a singular and engaging experience. It's a chance to escape the rush of modern society and restore a connection with the planet in its purest form
