WHITE VILLAGE OF FRIGILIANA





The beautiful white village of Frigiliana is hidden away among the rolling hills of Andalusia. Its whitewashed homes and winding cobblestone streets provide a peaceful getaway into the heart of rural Spain, where time seems to stand still. The village invites me to wander through its narrow alleys and take in the genuine beauty of its picturesque surroundings, with every turn revealing a new angle on the timeless charm of Spanish village life.
